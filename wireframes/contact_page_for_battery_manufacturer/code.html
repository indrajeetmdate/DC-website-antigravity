Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our process.

What is your typical lead time for custom modules? keyboard_arrow_down
For standard prototypes, we typically deliver within 4-6 weeks. Full-scale production timelines vary based on volume and complexity, but usually range from 3-4 months after design freeze.
Do you offer on-site integration support? keyboard_arrow_down
Yes, our engineering team provides on-site integration support for key partners during the initial deployment phase to ensure optimal system performance and safety compliance.
What certifications do your batteries hold? keyboard_arrow_down
Our modules are UN38.3, UL1973, and IEC62619 certified. We can also assist with specific regional compliance requirements depending on your target market.
